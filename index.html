About Me

My name is Anani Sam. I was born in Dambai in the Oti Region of Ghana. I spent my childhood at Dambai with my parents who were into fishing and farming. I now live in Ho in the Volta Region with my wife and a son. I am a High School teacher of over eighteen years of experience. I love swimming and love to learn new things.

Ho, Ghana

Ho is the capital city of the Volta Region of Ghana. The city lies between Mount Adaklu and Mount Galenukui, and is home to a Museum, a cathedral, an airport and at least, three universities. With a population of over hundred thousand people, the city has a beautiful scenery.
